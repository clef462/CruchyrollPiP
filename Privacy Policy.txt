Privacy Policy for Enable PiP Mode Extension

Effective Date: August 18, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the "Enable PiP Mode" browser extension. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

2. Information We Collect

The "Enable PiP Mode" extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not interact with any external servers or databases.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no user data that we use, share, or process. The extension only interacts with the video elements on web pages to enable the Picture-in-Picture (PiP) mode. All operations are performed locally on your device.

4. Permissions

The extension requires access to the following permissions:

Host Permissions (http://*/*, https://*/*): This permission is necessary to allow the extension to modify video elements on all websites, enabling the Picture-in-Picture mode. No data is collected or transmitted; the extension only modifies the attributes of video elements locally on the web pages you visit.
5. Third-Party Services

The "Enable PiP Mode" extension does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs. As such, no data is shared with third-party entities.

6. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Although our extension does not collect or store any personal data, we take the responsibility of operating in a secure manner seriously, ensuring that our extension does not introduce any vulnerabilities to your browsing experience.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
